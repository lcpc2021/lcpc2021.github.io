The Electrical and Computer Engineering Department and the Computer and Information Science Department at the University of Delaware are proud to host the 22nd annual LCPC workshop.

This workshop covers all aspects of languages, compiler techniques, run-time environments, and compiler-related performance evaluation for parallel and high-performance computing. Since 1988, the annual LCPC workshops have provided a forum for leading researchers and practitioners to present their latest work and to exchange ideas about future directions.
